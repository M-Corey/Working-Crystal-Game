Boring Box Matcher

Rules! Please dont break them.

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. There are four very boring looking boxes. By clicking on a box you will add a unknow amount of points to your total score. You win the game by matching your total score to the ramdomly generated number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each boring looking boxes is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each box.
